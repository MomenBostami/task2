90's Burger

90's Burger is one of the most renowned restaurants in the city of Nablus, especially celebrated for its delectable fast food, with a particular emphasis on burgers. Their menu pays homage to the classic fast food and comfort dishes of that era. From their mouthwatering burgers, generously stacked with fresh ingredients and an array of tantalizing sauces, to their crispy golden fries, 90's Burger offers a delightful journey through the flavors of yesteryears. and It is considered one of the most popular restaurants due to its excellence in its treatment, service, and the food it serves.
